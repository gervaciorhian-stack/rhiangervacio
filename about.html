Maffin Bakery

Welcome to Maffin Bakery, founder by Rhian Gervacio fresly Baked with happiness

History

It all started in 2015, when our founder Rhian Gervacio turned her family’s kitchen into a small baking space, making muffins and bread to share with neighbors in her hometown of Pasig City. What began as a way to use up ripe mangoes and coconut from her backyard quickly grew—friends started placing orders, and soon Rhian was waking up at 3 AM to keep up with demand. The name “Maffin Bakery” came from a playful mix-up: a young customer once excitedly asked for “maffins” instead of muffins, and the name stuck, reminding us to keep things light, fun, and focused on the joy our treats bring.

By 2017, we moved into a small storefront, bringing in Rhian’s younger brother Juan to help perfect our classic Filipino recipes and develop new creations. We kept our roots close, using family formulas for pandesal and ensaymada while adding our signature twist—like ube cream cheese muffins and coconut pandan bread that became instant hits. Our community supported us every step of the way, and we began donating leftover baked goods to the local elementary school, a tradition we still hold today.

In 2020, when the world changed, we adapted quickly—launching our website to bring our treats directly to families’ homes across Metro Manila. We spent months making sure our online ordering was simple and our delivery kept everything fresh and warm, just like picking it up from our shop. We also expanded our menu to include more dietary options, listening closely to customers who asked for gluten-free and vegan choices that didn’t compromise on taste.

Today, Maffin Bakery has grown to serve customers across the Philippines, but we still bake every item by hand with the same care Rhian put into her first batch of muffins. Our team feels like family, many of whom started as customers who fell in love with our work. We stay connected to our community by partnering with local farmers, hosting monthly baking workshops for kids, and continuing to give back to organizations that matter to us.

As we look ahead, we carry the spirit of that first kitchen with us—where every treat was made with love, every customer was a friend, and every day was a chance to bring a little sweetness to someone’s life. That’s the heart of Maffin Bakery, and it always will be.

Vision

We picture our bakery as the kind of place that feels like home—where the warm, cozy smell of bread rising in the oven greets you each morning and makes your day just a little brighter. We dream of sharing our love for Filipino baking with the whole country, keeping classics like pandesal and bibingka alive for our kids and grandkids, while also dreaming up new flavors that make people excited about local food. We want to show the world that businesses can do well by doing good—using ingredients grown with care, finding clever ways to use every last bit of what we have, and making sure we leave our planet better than we found it. Whether you’re scrolling through our website late at night or popping into our shop on your way to work, we want you to feel like you belong here—like we’re friends helping you find the perfect treat for your day. We’re working to make sure no one has to say “I wish I could eat that”—creating delicious options for every way of eating, because everyone deserves something special they can truly enjoy. We see ourselves growing up with you—remembering your child’s first birthday cake, helping you celebrate promotions, and being there for every milestone in between. We want to open our doors to anyone who loves baking as much as we do, teaching skills that can build careers and inspiring people to think more about where their food comes from and who makes it. Even as we share our treats with more families across the Philippines through our website, we’ll never forget where we started—rooted in our community, using local ingredients, and staying true to what makes our baking special. We believe a bakery should give back as much as it takes, so we’ll keep supporting our farmers, donating to neighbors who need a hand, and bringing people together over something as simple as good bread. At the end of the day, we don’t just want to be remembered for our pastries—we want to be the bakery that made you feel cared for, that brought your community closer, and that proved there’s so much heart in something as simple as baking.

Mission

We put our hands and hearts into every single thing we bake—from crusty loaves of bread to delicate pastries and celebratory cakes—because we believe fresh, quality ingredients make all the difference in how something tastes and makes you feel. We’re proud of our Filipino roots, so we keep beloved classics like pandesal and bibingka just as you remember them, while also having fun mixing in local flavors like ube and coconut to create new treats you’ll love. We work side by side with farmers and makers right here in our community, not just because it’s better for the planet, but because we want to support the hardworking people who help us do what we do. Your safety matters more than anything to us, so we hold ourselves to the highest standards in our kitchen—clean, careful, and consistent every single day. We hate wasting food or creating unnecessary trash, so we find ways to give leftover ingredients new life, wrap everything in packaging that’s kind to the earth, and only make what we know will be enjoyed. We built our website to be as easy to use as walking into our shop—so you can quickly find what you’re craving, add your own special touches, and get your order on its way without any hassle. We know everyone eats differently, so we’ve created gluten-free, vegan, and sugar-conscious options that taste just as amazing as our regular baked goods—because no one should have to miss out on something delicious. Whether you’re shopping online or stopping by in person, we treat you like part of our family—we listen to what you have to say, remember your favorite order, and make sure you feel seen and valued. We believe in sharing the joy of baking, which is why we donate to local schools, charities, and community events—because everyone deserves a little something sweet to brighten their day. At the end of the day, we don’t just want to be the place you order from—we want to be the bakery you turn to for morning coffee treats, birthday surprises, and every moment in between, bringing a little warmth and happiness to your table.
